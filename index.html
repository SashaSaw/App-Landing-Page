The Problem

You know what you should be doing.
Your phone has other plans.

📱

The 45-minute trap

You pick up your phone to check one thing. 45 minutes later you're deep in Instagram, TikTok, or Reddit. The habits you actually care about keep getting pushed to "tomorrow."

🚫

Blockers stop you, then what?

App blockers might keep you off Instagram, but they don't give you something better to do. You're left staring at a wall with no direction. Willpower alone isn't a plan.

📋

Trackers are another chore

Existing habit trackers are something you have to remember to open. They don't meet you in the moment of weakness, when you're reaching for your phone and need a nudge.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🔒

Smart App Blocking

Choose which apps to block and set a daily schedule. When you try to open them, you see your habits instead. A deliberate double-tap override gives you a 5-minute window if you really need it.

☑️

Your Day at a Glance

Must-do habits, nice-to-do habits, and one-off tasks for today. Everything you need to do lives in one place. Swipe to cross things off like a journal.

🔥

Good Day Streaks

Complete all your must-dos and earn a "good day." String good days together to build a streak. It's not about being perfect. It's about more good days than bad.

📚

Habit Groups

"Exercise" might be gym, swimming, or a run. Group them together so completing any one counts. Perfect for flexible habits and creative hobbies with notes and photos.

⏱️

Focus Mode

Tap a habit from the blocked app screen and start a focus timer. Default 25 minutes. Your phone stays locked to distractions while you do the thing that actually matters.

📅

Monthly Overview

A calendar grid showing every day against every habit with ticks and crosses. Patterns become obvious. See which habits you're nailing and which keep slipping.
